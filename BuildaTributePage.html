Hatsune miku

a vocaloid singer

  • Hatsune Miku, sometimes called Miku Hatsune, officially code-named CV01, is a Vocaloid software voicebank developed by Crypton Future Media and is its official mascot character, being depicted as a sixteen-year-old girl with long, turquoise twintails.

  • Active from: 2007

  • Available in: Japanese; English; Chinese

  • Developer(s): Crypton Future Media

  • License: Proprietary (Vocaloid voice/software); Creative Commons BY-NC (character design)

  • Operating system: Microsoft Windows, macOS
